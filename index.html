PEST CONTROL: Notes without mice
Your mouse is useless. Press 'RETURN' to make a note. Press 'I' for more instructions.

Your mouse does nothing!
All the buttons you need to use this app...
'I'
Show and hide instructions (press 'I' to get back to your notes).
'RETURN'
Start a new note, or finish the one you're working on.
'BACKSPACE'
Delete the selected note.
'E'
Edit the selected note.
UP-ARROW
Scroll Up.
DOWN-ARROW
Scroll Down.
